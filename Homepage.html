Facts that makes us Unique..!

Toastmasters Club

Toastmasters is an international organization that operates clubs worldwide for the purpose of promoting communication, and it is a place where you can prove your public speaking skills. Toastmasters group is more kind of self help along with mentors all over the world. It takes time to undrestand how beautifully it works but you need to be committed from yourside too. It has well built structure to improve you public speaking and leadership skills.

Leadership Activities

KIET aim to not only educate their students and hand them a degree, but guide them to become leaders in their various fields. A good leader represents a great team. The main motive behind the Leader Activities is to take out the leadership ,qualities in the students.These type of activities can help to survive in their respective fields after Graduation.KIET always supports students to become a good leader and to manage the team and to learn the things that will help for their careers.

KIOT

Industrial visits are an integral part of the college curriculum for professional degrees, during that students visit companies and get insight working evironment of company.It also provides an opportunity to experience how will be the real-time work culture. While a lot of theoretical knowledge is available in books, students get exposure only when they have industrial visits. The main reason behind this, it lets student to know things practically through interaction.

Internships

An Internship enables you to gain first-hand exposure of working in the real world. It also allows students to harness the skill, knowledge and theoritical practice they learned in University. An Internship can help to shape your career Trajectory, Expand your Professional networ and build your resume. KIET provides a lot of opportunities to do Internships when you are in Graduation.

Technology Training

Technology is constantly updating at such a rapid pace that it seems it is might be faster than light.! Learning and enhancing your skills are vital in this technological era. Here is the list of top new and trending technologies: Artificial Intelligence,Machine learning Data Science, Robotics, Cloud etch., These technologies have the potential to bring the learning to the student. KIET provides special training on the new technologies that will help to student to grow their knowledge.

Abhiyaan

We have all heard the saying "It takes 21 days to form a new Habit.." Abhiyaan is a 21-day program conducted every year especially for freshers to motivate their skills and knowledge and also to cheerup their upcoming four years journey with the college.so college came up with this program students should start doing to become better motivated, healthy, and successful durig the semester and years to come. These programs are adorable, fun, and relaxing!
